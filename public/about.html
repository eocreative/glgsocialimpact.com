What is GLG Social Impact

GLG helps top social sector leaders and organizations around the world accelerate their impact and scale by providing pro-bono and discounted memberships to GLG’s learning community. GLG Social Impact clients include leading nonprofits, social enterprises, foundations, and impact investing funds. GLG Social Impact advances learning and decision-making among the social sector’s top innovators – from supporting grantmaking and mission-driven investing through industry analysis and market landscaping to helping nonprofits with technical, regulatory, and strategic challenges.
