ABOUT GLG SOCIAL IMPACT FELLOWS

The Gerson Lehrman Group (GLG) is the world’s largest membership-based network for professional learning and expertise. With 20 offices globally and over 1,000 clients, GLG partners with organizations seeking targeted connections to insights by providing access to a global network of over 350,000 experts.

GLG Impact is an initiative that supports high-performing nonprofits and their leaders at critical growth junctures.
GLG Impact is part accelerator, part fellowship:

Accelerator
GLG Social Impact enables fellows and their senior management teams to accelerate the growth and impact of their organizations by leveraging our global network for unlimited, on-demand consultative services in partnership with GLG Research.
Fellowship
Leaders of high-impact nonprofits join the GLG community and receive dynamic mentorship and counsel from business, policy, and academic experts to improve their leadership capacity and extend their networks.

WHO IS IN THE GLG NETWORK

Experts in the GLG network, called Council Members, join the GLG community to teach, to share and to learn. Council Members participate in GLG Social Impact to share their knowledge and skills with high-performing social sector leaders and to help solve global challenges within the areas of their expertise

  • Academics, physicians, and research scientists
  • Policy, legal and regulatory Experts

WHO ARE GLG SOCIAL IMPACT FELLOWS?

The strongest candidates for the GLG Social Impact Fellows Program are passionate and tireless leaders of nonprofits and social enterprises with innovative solutions to create positive social change. Organizations considered must have between $750,000 - $10M in annual operating budget and focus aligned with one of the following:

  • Technology-enabled education
  • Professional development for underserved populations
  • Dissemination of expertise and knowledge
  • Social entrepreneurship
  • Leveraging networks for greater good
