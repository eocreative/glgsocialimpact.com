ABOUT GLG SOCIAL IMPACT FELLOWS

The GLG Social Impact Fellows program leverages our global network of 400,000 experts to support nonprofits and social enterprises at critical growth junctures, and their leaders, for a one year period. The GLG network supports you as an individual and your organization as a whole.


Part Accelerator
Fellows and their senior management teams accelerate the growth and impact of their organizations by leveraging our global network for unlimited, on-demand consultative services.
Part Fellowship
Leaders of nonprofits and social enterprises join the GLG community and receive dynamic mentorship and counsel from business, policy, and academic experts to expand their leadership capacity and networks.

HOW GLG SOCIAL IMPACT ACCELERATES PROGRESS

As a leader of an organization committed to a social mission over financial profit, you’re often operating with limited time and resources and wrestling with a range of questions.

Accelerator

  • Step one is partner with GLG Research to develop roadmap for how GLG will help you achieve your organization’s strategic and operational goals in the short and longer term.

  • Step two is access GLG proprietary technology platform to manage GLG resources.

  • Throughout the year, you and senior management team partner with dedicated, on-call GLG Research Consultant to identify and connect with our global network of experts for perspectives, insights and expertise – and custom-built solutions for your challenges and opportunities.

  • Common challenges we can help you with:

    Optimize operations and increase productivity

    Replicate and scale

    Understand legislation, trends and policies

    Enhance impact measurement programs

    Gather and analyze data to inform funding proposals and programming

    Shape pitches, advocacy and marketing campaigns

    Expand into new geographies

    Develop and refine new products

Fellowship

  • Expand your network and cultivate strategic relationships facilitated by GLG with senior executives and experts in the private, public and non-profit sectors.

  • Participate in virtual and in-person events with content curated specifically for GLG Social Impact Fellows.

  • Participate in GLG events held around the world, throughout the year – virtually and in-person – for our +1,000 clients.

WHO IS IN THE GLG NETWORK

Experts participate in GLG Social Impact to share their knowledge and skills with high-performing social sector leaders. Our network of 350,000 Council Members includes:

  • Fortune 500 executives
  • Industry participants across the global economy
    • Technology, Energy, Education, Telecom, Healthcare, Industrials, etc
  • Academics, physicians, engineers and research scientists
  • Policy, legal and regulatory experts

WHO ARE GLG SOCIAL IMPACT FELLOWS?

GLG Social Impact Fellows are curious, passionate and tireless leaders of nonprofits and social enterprises. They have a strong record and clear vision for their organizations.

Their organizations:

  • Have innovative models creating demonstrable social change
  • Between 2-10 years old
  • Have between $500,000 - $10M in annual operating budget
  • Multi-year plans in place with discrete goals
