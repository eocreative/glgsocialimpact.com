ABOUT GLG SOCIAL IMPACT FELLOWS

The Gerson Lehrman Group (GLG) is the world’s largest membership-based network for professional learning and expertise. With 20 offices globally and over 1,000 clients, GLG partners with organizations seeking targeted connections to insights by providing access to a global network of over 350,000 experts.

The GLG Social Impact Fellows program supports high-performing nonprofits and their leaders at critical growth junctures.

Part Accelerator
Fellows and their senior management teams accelerate the growth and impact of their organizations by leveraging our global network for unlimited, on-demand consultative services.
Part Fellowship
Leaders of high-impact nonprofits join the GLG community and receive dynamic mentorship and counsel from business, policy, and academic experts to expand their leadership capacity and networks.

  • Common challenges we can help you with:

    Optimize operations and increase productivity

    Replicate and scale

    Understand legislation, trends and policies

    Gather and analyze data to inform funding proposals and programming

    Develop impact measurement programs

    Expand into new geographies

Fellowship

WHO ARE GLG SOCIAL IMPACT FELLOWS?

GLG Social Impact Fellows are passionate and tireless leaders of nonprofits and social enterprises.

Their organizations:

  • Have innovative solutions for positive social change
  • Have between $750,000 - $10M in annual operating budget
  • One of the following focus areas:
    • Technology-enabled education
    • Professional development for underserved populations
    • Dissemination of expertise and knowledge
    • Social entrepreneurship
    • Leveraging networks for greater good
