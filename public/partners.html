Partnerships

Since our company's founding 15 years ago, we have partnered with leading social sector organizations around the world. Below are some of the organizations which have leveraged GLG's network to enhance and accelerate impact.

Spirit of America is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping Americans serving abroad assist local people in need by providing materials and expertise on the ground. Please see a case study and op-ed about our partnership.

The Resolution Project, Inc. is dedicated to fostering youth leadership development around the world through collaborative social entrepreneurship.

Endeavor Global is a nonprofit that seeks to transform emerging economies by supporting high-impact entrepreneurs. Please see a blog post about our partnership.

The Bridgespan Group is a nonprofit advisor and resource for mission-driven organizations and philanthropists.

Echoing Green is a nonprofit that provides seed funding and technical assistance to emerging social entrepreneurs with ideas for social change

The Partnership Fund for New York City identifies and supports New York City’s most promising entrepreneurs in both the for-profit and not-for-profit sectors.

The Juilliard School is a performing arts conservatory which currently trains undergraduate and graduate students in dance, drama, and music.

LIFT’s mission is to help community members achieve economic stability and well-being. LIFT is working to establish a new standard for holistic and enduring solutions in our country’s fight against poverty.

Youth INC is a venture philanthropy organization that invests in nonprofits serving children in the New York City area. 

Khosla Impact is a venture fund that invests in market-based solutions and assists entrepreneurs developing products and services for people at the bottom of the world’s economic pyramid.

OneGoal identifies, trains and supports our nation's most effective teachers to lead underperforming high school students to reach their full potential and graduate from college.

The Wadhwani Foundation is a private grant-making foundation committed to improving standards of living in emerging economies through accelerating economic development.

Bpeace is a non-profit network of business professionals who volunteer skills to entrepreneurs in conflict-affected countries to help them create significant employment for all.
