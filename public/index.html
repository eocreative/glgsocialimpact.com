POWERING SOCIAL IMPACT

Gerson Lehrman Group (GLG) is the world’s largest membership-based network for professional learning and expertise. We partner with organizations seeking targeted connections to insights by providing access to a global network of over 350,000 experts.

Through GLG Social Impact, we facilitate the dissemination and exchange of expertise to leading social sector organizations to accelerate growth and impact.

Impact Fellows

GLG Impact Fellows are high-potential nonprofit leaders and social entrepreneurs who recognize the value of expertise to their organizations' growth and impact.

Impact Partners

GLG Impact Partners are nonprofits, foundations, impact investors and social enterprises which leverage GLG's network for access to unique domain expertise and perspective.
