POWERING SOCIAL IMPACT

Gerson Lehrman Group (GLG) is the world’s largest membership-based network for professional learning and expertise. We partner with organizations seeking targeted connections to insights by providing access to a global network of over 350,000 experts.

Through GLG Impact, we partner with individuals and organizations committed to creating positive social change on a local and global level. We pair changemakers with leading professionals in our network - from domestic and international policy specialists to economic advisors, business leaders to academics, doctors to engineers - to ask questions, acquire insights and test ideas. GLG Impact facilitates the dissemination and exchange of expertise to accelerate social impact.
