POWERING SOCIAL IMPACT

Gerson Lehrman Group (GLG) is the world’s largest membership-based network for professional learning and expertise. We partner with organizations seeking targeted connections to insights by providing access to a global network of over 350,000 experts.

Through GLG Social Impact, we connect social sector organizations with experts across industries and geographies for perspectives and expertise to accelerate the impact of their work.

Impact Fellows

GLG Impact Fellows are high-potential nonprofit leaders and social entrepreneurs who join the GLG community for a one year period.
